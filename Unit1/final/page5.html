Tips and Tricks!

Tips Why
Learn Simple French Phrases! A simple "Bonjour," "Merci," and "S'il vous plaît" will be greatly appreciated.
Watch for pickpocketers. Stay alert in tourist areas and crowded places, as pickpocketing is common.
Rent Wisely If renting a car for exploring smaller towns, choose a smaller model due to narrow roads and tight parking.
Go With the Flow! Thing are constantly getting closed due to strikes, so don't get too frustrated when your plans don't come through! Have backup plans!

7 Day Itinerary Example!

Day 1: Arrive in Paris

  • Arrive at Charles de Gaulle Airport.
  • Check into hotel and relax after the flight. Don't be in a rush!
  • Visit the Eiffel Tower — book tickets in advance for sunset view.
  • Dinner at Café Constant for a classic French meal.

Day 2: Paris to Loire Valley

  • Take a TGV train from Paris Montparnasse to Tours (~1h15) or rent a car for more flexibility.
  • Visit Loire Valley Château. Look around and learn about the rich history.
  • Stop for lunch at Auberge du Bon Terroir.
  • Walk the old town, visit Leonardo da Vinci’s last home (Clos Lucé).
  • Check into hotel (eg; Amboise) and have dinner in the hotel.

Day 3:Loire Valley to French Alps

  • Early drive or return to Tours for TGV to Annecy (5-6 hours). Breakfast and lunch are eaten on train.
  • Arrive in Annecy, the "Venice of the Alps."
  • Stroll through Old Town, admire canals, lakeside views, and the Palais de l’Isle.
  • Try local food for dinner at Savoyard cuisine.
  • Overnight stay at Chamonix.

Day 4:Exploring the French Alps

  • Rent bikes and cycle along Lake Annecy. If you're feeling more adventurous, rent paddle boats to row in the lake.
  • Visit Roc de Chère Nature Reserve.
  • Explore small shops and explore new foods.
  • End of the day with some hiking in the many trails.

Day 5:French Alps to Côte D'Azur (Nice)

  • Take the TVG to go to Nice (~6 hours)
  • Arrive in Nice, check into a hotel near the Old Town or Promenade des Anglais.
  • Buy some snacks and stroll the Cours Saleya market and beach promenade.
  • Dinner in Vieux Nice — try local dishes like socca or pissaladière.

Day 6:Exploring Côte D'Azur (Nice)

  • Train to Monaco (~30 mins).
  • Visit the Casino de Monte Carlo, the Prince’s Palace, and Oceanographic Museum.
  • Stop at Èze Village on the return and visit the Fragonard perfume factory. Walk the exotic gardens!
  • Return to Nice and have a sunset dinner at Le Plongeoir.

Day 7:Leaving

  • Enjoy a relaxing breakfast.
  • Go on a nice swim or walk along the Mediterranean.
  • Head to Nice Côte d’Azur Airport for your flight.
